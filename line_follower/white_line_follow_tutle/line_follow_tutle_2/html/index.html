Connections:

Direction Pins of Motors are connected to the ATmega328P microcontroller.
Motors Pin Microcontroller Pin
RB --> PD7
RF --> PD6
LF --> PD2
LB --> PD4

PWM or Enable Pins of Motors of the ATmega328P microcontroller.
PWM Pin Micro controller Pin
Left Motor --> PD3
Right Motor --> PD5
